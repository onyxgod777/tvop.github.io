The Golden Ratio ($\Phi$) appears throughout the natural world in ways that suggest it is a fundamental organizing principle.


## Examples
### Sunflower Seed Heads
The number of clockwise and counterclockwise spirals in sunflower seed heads are typically consecutive Fibonacci numbers, whose ratio approaches $\Phi$.


### Nautilus Shells
The logarithmic spiral of the nautilus shell follows the golden ratio growth pattern.


### Hurricane Spirals
Satellite images of hurricanes show spiral structures that approximate golden spiral proportions.


### Human Body

- The ratio of forearm to hand length approximates $\Phi$
- The ratio of total height to navel height approximates $\Phi$
- Finger joint proportions follow the golden ratio
