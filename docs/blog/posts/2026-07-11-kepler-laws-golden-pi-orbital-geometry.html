The Paperclip Orbit as a Golden Rectangle Condition

The perimeter consists of two semicircular arcs contributed by each semicircle.

⟹ A = πa²/8 · φ

With π = 4/√φ:

The math finishes cleanly. Under conventional π, the composite area remains transcendental and never reproduces the golden-rectangle expression algebraically.
