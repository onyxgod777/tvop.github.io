2. Vision & Philosophy

The current memecoin landscape is dominated by insider deals, pre-sales to VCs, and team allocations that allow developers to dump on retail. Pump.fun was created to solve this, but even on fair-launch platforms, most tokens lack transparency, community engagement, or long-term vision.

2.2 The $CLAW Solution

$CLAW is built on three pillars:

  • Fair Launch — Every buyer, including the creator, buys on the open bonding curve at market price. No tokens are pre-mined, pre-allocated, or reserved.
  • Transparency — All token movements, burns, and holdings are publicly visible on Solana's blockchain. The deployer wallet is disclosed.
  • Community Governance — Future decisions about the token's direction, marketing, and development are made collectively by holders.

$CLAW is called the "Decentralized Intelligence Token" because it represents the collective intelligence of its community. 4. Bonding Curve Mechanics

$CLAW launched on pump.fun's automated bonding curve. The bonding curve is a mathematical pricing mechanism where the price of tokens increases as more tokens are purchased.

4.1 How It Works

  • The first buyer purchases tokens at the lowest possible price
  • Each subsequent buy increases the price for the next buyer
  • Sellers can exit at any time — the curve always has liquidity
  • No impermanent loss or complex LP management required

4.2 Raydium Migration

When the bonding curve reaches a market cap of approximately $69,000 (100% curve completion), the accumulated liquidity is automatically migrated to Raydium, a decentralized exchange on Solana. This transition provides:

  • Access to a wider trading audience via Jupiter aggregator
  • Potential listing on DexScreener and DexTools
  • Deepened liquidity through Raydium's AMM pools

6. Fair Launch Principles

$CLAW adheres to strict fair launch principles:

  • No Presale: No tokens were sold before the public launch.
  • No Team Allocation: The creator holds no pre-allocated tokens. Any tokens held by the deployer were purchased on the open bonding curve at market price, just like any other buyer.
  • No Hidden Wallets: All wallets associated with the project are publicly disclosed.
  • Equal Access: Every buyer has equal opportunity to purchase at the same bonding curve price.
  • Verifiable Supply: Total supply, circulating supply, and burn amounts are fully verifiable on Solana's block explorer.
