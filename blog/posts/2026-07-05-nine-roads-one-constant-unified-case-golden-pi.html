Why Convergence Is the Real Proof

A skeptic can always attack one derivation. The Kepler triangle depends on reading the right angle correctly. The pentagon depends on choosing chord-versus-arc as the comparison. Convergence defeats that strategy. The assumptions differ. The goals differ. The languages differ. Yet every path terminates at the same algebraic expression: π = 4/√φ. In a universe of independent experiments, five coincidences look like luck. Nine look like structure.

The nested pattern: Pentagrams nest inside pentagons; spirals nest inside chambers; harmonic overtones nest inside fundamental frequencies; Platonic solids nest inside circumscribed spheres; Planck and electron scales nest inside the fine-structure constant. Because every nesting step is governed by φ or φ-related ratios, the circular boundary that must close each step cannot speak a different algebraic language. It must speak φ as well, and the only φ-consistent circle constant is 4/√φ.
